Recommended Getaway: Honolulu, Hawaii

The best time to visit Hawaii is between March and September. This is when the islands see the highest temperatures and the lowest amount of rain. It's the perfect time to enjoy the beach or the water.

Recommended Getaway: Miami, Florida

The best time to visit Florida is in the spring and fall. Both periods offer the best weather with warm temperatures and less rainfall. The south has the best combination of sunny skies, warm waters, and hot temperatures.

Recommended Getaway: Queenstown, New Zealand

In July the ski season is in full swing, so this is a fantastic time to visit Queenstown. Even if you're not a skier, these regions are stunning under a dusting of snow

Recommended Getaway: Denver, Colorado

The best time to visit Denver is generally from April through May and September through October. Moderate weather and affordable hotel rates can be expected during these shoulder seasons.
